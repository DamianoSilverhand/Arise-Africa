Young Africans in Action


Service Above Self

African youths have taken it upon themselves to uplift the standards of their communities, especially in areas of community development, social welfare and entreprenuership. These Youths are changing lives through activities aimed at identifying brilliant young Africans to take up roles in community leadership, to lead the dialogue on development, get involved in civil society organizations or their professional activities.

These youths have organised themselves in different associations and organizations depending on their approach to better help their communities.

Not For Profit Organizations

Not-for-profit, non governmental organizations such as African Youth Development Foundation and Asante Africa Foundation mobilize resources and promote and support sustainable youth empowerment and aim to empower the next generation of change agents , whose dreams and actions transform the future of Africa and the world.

Social Enterprises

These organizations seek to maximize profits while maximizing benefits to society and the environment. Their profits are principally used to fund social programs. Empact of Youth Organizations

The empact of the Youth organizations is being noticed in the communities, this is mainly because the organizations understand the needs and challenges of fellow youths better and are doing something to change the lives of fellow youths for the better.
